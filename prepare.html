Clear the areas being cleaned

We're going to be moving around some big machines! So, we need as much space as possible to keep the floor looking clean. Before we come to scrub the floors, please be sure to move anything that could be in the way.

Double check to make sure nothing is blocking where we need to go, either. Aisles that have no entrance have no way of getting clean!

Keep the lights on!

It's hard to work in the dark. So, if possible, please leave the lights on nights when we're going to clean. Or, be sure we are able to turn the lights off and on ourselves.

Let us know about big messes

Some surprises are not fun! Before we start cleaning, we need to know if there's any areas that need special attention. And, certain messes need certain materials in order to clean them up. We can do our job much better if we know about any special needs.
